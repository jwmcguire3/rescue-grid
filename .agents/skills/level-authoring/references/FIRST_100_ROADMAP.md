# First 100 Roadmap

This roadmap is for production campaign planning beyond the Phase 1 L00-L15 prototype packet. Do not treat it as a fixed list of implemented levels.

Use this file to infer reasonable defaults when designing new production levels or packets.

## Campaign arc

### Levels 1-10: Rescue literacy

Make the player understand that rescue, not clearing, is the point. Emphasize valid group clears, dock basics, visible target progress, first rescue extraction, water as action-based pressure, and first rescue-order choice.

### Levels 11-20: Dock discipline

Make the dock feel dangerous but self-authored. Emphasize group-size consequences, triple timing, route clears that cost dock space, and tempting broad clears that harm rescue tempo.

### Levels 21-30: Ice and future value

Teach that some moves are valuable because of what they reveal. Emphasize ice on rescue routes, adjacency literacy, future-value reads, and blocker choice as rescue-order support.

### Levels 31-40: Water rescue order

Make water sharpen target priority. Emphasize lower endangered targets, wrong-order consequences, route race without real-time pressure, and readable water forecast.

### Levels 41-50: Vine pressure

Make vine feel warned, authored, and attributable. Emphasize visible future route tax, clipping vine at the right time, and choosing between water urgency and vine growth.

### Levels 51-60: Mixed blockers

Combine crates, ice, and vines while keeping one readable problem. Emphasize blocker sequencing, route prioritization, and dock cost of blocker-adjacent clears.

### Levels 61-70: Multi-target triage

Make target order the central puzzle. Emphasize two- and three-target tradeoffs, false-easy targets, partial progress that can be a trap, and clear extraction order telemetry.

### Levels 71-80: Dock-rescue integration

Make the best rescue route also require dock discipline. Emphasize route actions that create risky dock remainder, safer clears that cost tempo, and final rescue exception clarity.

### Levels 81-90: Mastery remixes

Remix known rules in fresh geometries. Emphasize strong first reads, varied board shapes, recovery beats between harder tests, and fair but less signposted choices.

### Levels 91-100: Campaign climax

Deliver memorable, readable rescue drama using the full known ruleset. Emphasize high-stakes but legible target order, satisfying rescue moves, emotionally clear extraction, and spectacle levels that still play honestly.
